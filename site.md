# AI Website Builder Prompt – Markapuram Digital Development Portal

## Objective
Build a modern, scalable, AI-powered digital development portal focused on Markapuram District, Andhra Pradesh. The platform should serve citizens by providing transparent, timely, and easy-to-understand information about public development, government schemes, rehabilitation progress, employment opportunities, local events, and civic services. It should also support volunteer coordination and content management.

The portal must prioritize factual accuracy, clearly identify information sources, distinguish verified information from opinion, and avoid publishing unverified or misleading claims. AI should assist with drafting and summarization, while allowing editorial review before publication where appropriate.

# AI Content Engine
Automatically gather information from trusted and publicly available sources.

Examples include:

- Official Government of Andhra Pradesh websites
- District administration websites
- Official department press releases
- Public notifications
- Gazette publications
- Approved public datasets
AI should:

- Detect newly published content
- Summarize articles
- Translate between Telugu and English
- Generate SEO-friendly draft blog posts
- Generate FAQs
- Categorize articles
- Suggest tags
- Recommend related articles

↓

# Goals
Build a trusted, transparent, citizen-focused digital platform that makes public information easier to access, improves awareness of government services, highlights verified development updates, and helps citizens engage constructively with local administration. The platform should be scalable, secure, multilingual, AI-assisted, and maintain clear editorial standards for accuracy and transparency.
